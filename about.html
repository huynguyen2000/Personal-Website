Who I Am
Hi. My name is Huy Nguyen and I am currently a junior working on my computer science degree. I was born in Vietnam and my family decided to immigrate to the US in 2007. When I was in high school, I actually had no interest in the field of computer science. I remember not really knowing what I wanted to do and was leaning towards the medical field. I had always been good with math and logic and so when I went to college at TAMU, I declared my major as Biochemical Engineering since it involved both health and math. From there, it was actually the general engineering class that first introduced me to coding via Python, and ever since then, I knew exactly what I wanted to do. I switched over to CS for my major and never looked back.
Some of my interests outside of coding consists mostly of games and playing guitar. I've been playing the accoustic guitar for about 3-4 years now and I find that it's a really good way to pass the time and destress. As for games, I enjoy a lot of strategy and card games and it's also a way for me to connect with my friends.
